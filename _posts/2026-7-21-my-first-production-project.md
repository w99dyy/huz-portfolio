I was lucky that my second ever project had to be for a small art community and it was put to prodcution,
I learned and improved a lot when I started working on this project, I learned how to put a web app
to production, how to SSH into VPS, understanding Databases and Docker (I only layed my hands on a small fragment
 of knowledge). This was a great opportunity for a self-taught developer like me,
that's why I consider my self lucky.


While it was a simple CRUD web app but it took me more than 4 month to push it into production, The reason
behind that was I'm a BACKEND DEVELOPER, I didn't know much about forum design so I had to get multiple
references and because of that I had to change the UI a lot during the process, but overall it was so much fun
working on this project, I always found forums interesting and cool.
